Theorem 10.2.4.

The alternating group, \(A_n\text{,}\) is simple for \(n \geq 5\text{.}\)

Proof.

Let \(N\) be a normal subgroup of \(A_n\text{.}\) By Lemma 10.2.3, \(N\) contains a \(3\)-cycle. By Lemma 10.2.2, \(N = A_n\text{;}\) therefore, \(A_n\) contains no proper nontrivial normal subgroups for \(n \geq 5\text{.}\)
