Lemma 6.1.3.

Let \(H\) be a subgroup of a group \(G\) and suppose that \(g_1, g_2 \in G\text{.}\) The following conditions are equivalent.
  1. \(g_1 H = g_2 H\text{;}\)
  2. \(H g_1^{-1} = H g_2^{-1}\text{;}\)
  3. \(g_1 H \subset g_2 H\text{;}\)
  4. \(g_2 \in g_1 H\text{;}\)
  5. \(g_1^{-1} g_2 \in H\text{.}\)
